Q&A for starting dev+ops engineers

This is a list of topics that someone working as a devops person could work through. Additionaly, there are some topics that deal with our infrastructure and production setup.

For each topic, you'll find a list of questions, short answers, exercises, and pointers to additional introductory reading material. Eventually! This is a work in progress.

This document assumes familiarity with basic programming but nothing else. The goal of this document is to provide enough information for someone to works through it to be able to find the rest of what they need to know for any service they develop, deploy and maintain, as well as understanding performance issues relating to the underlying OS, hardware or network.

One goal of this document is that you should be able to answer the following question:

What happens when you enter "https://en.wikipedia.org" in your browser address bar and hit enter?
